# Interactive Operation Control EARS Requirements

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## REQ-IC-013 — Intervention Boundaries

**EARS:** Where an operation supports intervention, the system shall permit an authorized user to pause or resume at declared checkpoints and shall identify completed, current, pending, and skipped work.

**Stories:** US-107

## REQ-IC-014 — Staged Activation and Continuation Conditions

**EARS:** When an Activation Plan declares Rollout Stages, the system shall activate only the current stage, evaluate its verification and continuation conditions, record the continuation decision, and prevent automatic expansion after a failed required check.

**Stories:** US-106
