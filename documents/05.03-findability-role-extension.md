# Findability Role Extension

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

- **Information Architect:** defines and evaluates Navigation Models, labels, facets, and cross-channel wayfinding.
- **Vocabulary Steward:** manages preferred labels, aliases, definitions, mappings, review dates, and supersession for an assigned scope.
- **Findability Evaluator:** maintains representative tasks and evaluates discovery, recognition, completion, recovery, accessibility, and retrieval behavior.
- **Workspace Owner:** saves and revises a task workspace, review checkpoint, view choices, and permitted preferences.
- **Configuration Steward:** proposes Management Change Plans, reviews Impact Previews, and initiates approved configuration or bulk changes.
- **Accessibility Reviewer:** evaluates navigation, refinement, previews, status, and management operations through supported interaction modes.

These roles are scoped and effective-dated. They do not automatically include authority to revise or approve source information.
