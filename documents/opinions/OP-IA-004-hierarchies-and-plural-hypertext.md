# Opinion Article OP-IA-004 — Hierarchy Is One View

**Status:** Interpretive
**Primary counterpoint:** Ted Nelson
**Associated principles:** IA-P-002, IA-P-004, IA-P-005
**Associated requirements:** REQ-IA-003, REQ-IA-004, REQ-IA-009, REQ-HT-008

## Position

Menus, breadcrumbs, and facets help people predict where information may be found.

## Nelson Counterpoint

Hypertext preserves authored links, alternatives, versions, and many legitimate paths. A tidy hierarchy can falsely imply that one placement or sequence is complete.

## Opinion

Use hierarchy for orientation while preserving backlinks, Relationships, Trails, exact source identity, and alternatives. Relabeling or moving an item must not erase authored connections or historical addresses.
