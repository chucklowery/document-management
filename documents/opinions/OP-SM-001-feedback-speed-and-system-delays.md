# Opinion Article OP-SM-001 — Fast Freshness Is Not the Same as Effective Feedback

**Status:** Interpretive

This article records the tension between fast propagation and stable, governed feedback. It is associated with SM-P-003, SM-P-009, SM-P-011, REQ-SM-008, REQ-OP-001, and REQ-OP-005.

A Donella H. Meadows counterpoint is that information flow and feedback delay are system leverage points: shortening a delay without understanding the loop may amplify noise and oscillation. The system should use consequence-specific propagation objectives, disclose delay, and preserve reproducible boundaries rather than demand one universal zero-latency target.
