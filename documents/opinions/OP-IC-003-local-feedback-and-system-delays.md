# Opinion Article OP-IC-003 — Feedback and Delay

**Status:** Interpretive  
**Counterpoint:** Donella H. Meadows  
**Associated principles:** IC-P-002, IC-P-005, IC-P-006  
**Associated requirements:** REQ-IC-007, REQ-IC-009, REQ-IC-010, REQ-IC-011, REQ-IC-012, REQ-IC-014

A Meadows-oriented counterpoint is that prompt local feedback may not reveal outcomes that appear after a delay.

The project should combine immediate interaction feedback with staged observation, capacity measures, and explicit review of later outcomes.
