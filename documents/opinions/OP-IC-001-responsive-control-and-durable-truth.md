# Opinion Article OP-IC-001 — Responsive Control and Durable Completion

**Status:** Interpretive  
**Primary lens:** Brad A. Myers-inspired interaction principles  
**Associated principles:** IC-P-001, IC-P-002, IC-P-003, IC-P-006, IC-P-008  
**Associated requirements:** REQ-IC-001, REQ-IC-004, REQ-IC-005, REQ-IC-010, REQ-IC-011, REQ-IC-012, REQ-IC-013, REQ-IC-016, REQ-IC-022

Rapid feedback helps users understand an action and correct mistakes. Durable processing and delivery may finish later, and prior history must remain available.

The system should acknowledge intent promptly, show progress, reserve completed language for the declared durable boundary, and offer restoration or follow-up work when a prior outcome cannot simply be removed.

Responsiveness and truthful completion are both required. Neither requirement cancels the other.
