# Derived-Store Security EARS Requirements

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## REQ-SC-001 — Authorization Before Derived Disclosure

**EARS:** Before the system indexes, retrieves, explains, caches, aggregates, embeds, exports, or delivers information, it shall apply the authorization, sensitivity, consent, retention, deletion, completeness-disclosure, and inference-risk policies required for that operation.

**Stories:** US-059, US-072, US-075

## REQ-SC-002 — Revocation Propagation Objective

**EARS:** When access, consent, retention, sensitivity, deletion, or legal policy changes, the system shall remove, restrict, invalidate, or cryptographically erase affected derived representations within the applicable policy deadline and shall expose unresolved propagation state to authorized stewards.

**Stories:** US-072

## REQ-SC-003 — Prevent Aggregate and Ordering Leakage

**EARS:** If a user is not authorized to know that information exists, then the system shall not disclose it through facets, counts, similarity neighborhoods, ranking gaps, result ordering, timing differences, diagnostics, cache behavior, or delivery metadata beyond policy.

**Stories:** US-072, US-075

## REQ-SC-004 — Protected Query and Feedback Data

**EARS:** When Queries, result interactions, or Relevance Judgments contain sensitive information, the system shall apply explicit collection, access, retention, use, and deletion policy and shall not reuse them for ranking outside the authorized purpose.

**Stories:** US-063, US-072

## REQ-SC-005 — Policy Version in Consequential Result

**EARS:** When a Retrieval Projection, Output Manifest, or Consistency Projection is used for a consequential action, the system shall identify the material authorization and disclosure policy versions applied.

**Stories:** US-060, US-071, US-072

## REQ-SC-006 — Verify Derived-Store Removal

**EARS:** When policy requires removal from derived stores, the system shall produce verification evidence for the applicable indexes, caches, facets, embeddings, replicas, exports, and pending deliveries or shall identify any permitted unresolved residue.

**Stories:** US-072
