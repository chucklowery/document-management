# Preview and Personalization Requirements

## REQ-IA-009 — Traceable Result Preview
**EARS:** When a Result Preview is shown, the system shall identify its source object and shall not present the preview as the complete source.
**Stories:** US-081

## REQ-IA-010 — Decision Credibility Signals
**EARS:** When information supports a consequential action, the system shall make authority, Revision or time boundary, freshness, derivative status, uncertainty, and material limitations available before confirmation.
**Stories:** US-081

## REQ-IA-011 — Explain Personalization
**EARS:** When personalization materially changes ordering, defaults, or recommendations, the system shall identify the applied Personalization Policy and affected presentation behavior.
**Stories:** US-083

## REQ-IA-012 — Reset Personalization
**EARS:** Where policy permits personalization, the system shall allow the user to reset or disable it without changing source authority or history.
**Stories:** US-083
