# Retrieval Acceptance Specifications

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

# Feature AS-021 — Authorized and Explainable Retrieval

**Stories:** US-059, US-060, US-075  
**Requirements:** REQ-IR-001 through REQ-IR-007; REQ-IR-015; REQ-SC-001; REQ-SC-003

## Scenario AS-021.1 — Ranked result identifies source and explanation

**Given** an authorized corpus contains several Resources and exact Revisions,  
**And** a Ranking Policy is active,  
**When** an Information Seeker submits a Query,  
**Then** every returned result identifies its managed object and applicable Revision or Region occurrence,  
**And** identifies authority, generated time, and freshness,  
**And** provides the principal permitted match and ranking factors,  
**And** does not present the ranking as the sole canonical structure.

## Scenario AS-021.2 — Restricted candidate does not leak

**Given** a restricted Resource would otherwise match a Query,  
**And** the user is not authorized to know that it exists,  
**When** the Query is evaluated,  
**Then** the Resource is not disclosed through content, identity, counts, facets, ordering gaps, timing detail, explanation, or errors.

## Scenario AS-021.3 — Delayed indexing is visible

**Given** an accepted Revision is eligible for search,  
**And** indexing has exceeded its objective,  
**When** an authorized user searches or inspects system status,  
**Then** the system exposes the delayed Consistency State and permitted limitation,  
**And** does not falsely claim the corpus is current or complete.

# Feature AS-022 — Historical Retrieval

**Stories:** US-061  
**Requirements:** REQ-IR-004; REQ-IR-008; REQ-IR-015

## Scenario AS-022.1 — Later information is excluded from historical search

**Given** Revision 1 existed at the requested recorded-time boundary,  
**And** Revision 2 was recorded later,  
**When** a reviewer searches as of the earlier boundary,  
**Then** Revision 1 is eligible according to policy,  
**And** Revision 2 is excluded,  
**And** the system identifies whether recorded time or effective time governed the search.

## Scenario AS-022.2 — Historical identity lookup remains exact

**Given** a historical citation identifies an exact Revision,  
**When** the reviewer resolves that identity,  
**Then** the system does not substitute a newer or more highly ranked Resource.
