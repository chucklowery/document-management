# Findability and Information Architecture Canonical Terms

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## Core Terms

**Information Need** — The task-centered statement of what a Party needs to locate, understand, compare, or act upon.

**Navigation Model** — A versioned definition of global, local, contextual, graph, Trail, chronological, and task-specific paths.

**Wayfinding Context** — The visible account of current scope, identity, Revision, arrival path, time boundary, active constraints, and available next paths.

**Vocabulary** — A versioned set of terms, definitions, aliases, context ownership, mappings, and lifecycle rules.

**Facet** — A governed dimension used to group or refine eligible information.
