# User Roles

Roles are contextual and may be held by people, teams, organizations, or authorized automated agents.

## Source Author
Creates and revises authoritative source content and identifies evidence, assumptions, and intended meaning.

## Source Owner
Governs authoritative revisions, reuse policy, withdrawal, correction, and downstream impact.

## Destination Author
Uses references, transclusions, summaries, and annotations in a destination Resource without acquiring ownership of the source.

## Destination Owner
Controls adoption of source changes into a working destination and accepts responsibility for destination context.

## Evidence Contributor
Provides observations, records, artifacts, measurements, or testimony while retaining attribution and applicable consent constraints.

## Synthesizer
Creates findings, summaries, and interpretations while preserving sources, transformations, omissions, disagreement, and uncertainty.

## Reviewer
Examines exact Revisions, Relationships, claims, and changes and may support, dispute, or request revision.

## Approver
Exercises explicit authority over publication, adoption, execution, disclosure, or another governed transition.

## Knowledge Steward
Maintains definitions, classifications, ownership, staleness, retention, and constitutional coverage without becoming the universal authority on meaning.

## Trail Author
Creates and maintains an ordered reading or reasoning path through connected Resources for a stated audience and purpose.

## Auditor
Reconstructs historical states, decisions, publications, derivations, access-sensitive actions, and verification evidence.

## Consent Grantor
Controls consent for collection, retention, reuse, disclosure, or portrayal where applicable.

## System Operator
Maintains availability, integrity, repair, migration, export, and operational policy enforcement.

## Automated Agent
Produces proposals, transformations, analyses, or generated outputs under explicit authority. It shall disclose governing instructions, material inputs, tool or model version, and limitations, and shall not silently redefine meaning or authority.
