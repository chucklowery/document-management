# Channel and Contract EARS Requirements

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## REQ-LO-001 — Managed Channel Catalog

**EARS:** When an input, output, event, synchronization, or delivery channel becomes managed, the system shall create a Channel Catalog Entry identifying owner, direction, authority, active Contract Revisions, dependencies, service objectives, retention, and lifecycle state.

**Stories:** US-091

## REQ-LO-002 — Contract Revision Selection

**EARS:** When information is accepted or delivered, the system shall identify the selected Input Contract or Output Contract Revision and shall reject, quarantine, or negotiate an unsupported Revision according to the channel policy.

**Stories:** US-092

## REQ-LO-003 — Compatibility Assessment

**EARS:** When a material Contract Revision is proposed, the system shall classify compatibility for identified producers and consumers using semantic and syntactic evidence and shall not treat unknown compatibility as compatible.

**Stories:** US-092

## REQ-LO-004 — Preserve Received Meaning and Time

**EARS:** When an input is mapped into managed information, the system shall preserve the received contract identity, source identity, source value or governed reference, mapping Revision, temporal values, timezone or offset, precision, and known clock uncertainty where material.

**Stories:** US-092, US-101

## REQ-LO-019 — Contract Conformance Suite

**EARS:** When a Contract Revision is proposed for adoption, the system shall run a versioned conformance suite containing representative valid, invalid, boundary, correction, retirement, ordering, retry, and compatibility examples and shall preserve the results used for adoption.

**Stories:** US-092
