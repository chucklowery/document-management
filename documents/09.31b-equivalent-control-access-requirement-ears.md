# Equivalent Control Access EARS Requirement

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## REQ-IC-023 — Equivalent Accessible Control

**EARS:** When a control, status, preview, validation finding, comparison, progress state, or recovery action is available, the system shall provide equivalent keyboard-operable and supported assistive-technology semantics consistent with REQ-IA-016.

**Stories:** US-102, US-104, US-107, US-111
