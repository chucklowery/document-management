# Feature AS-034 — Previewed Management Change

**Stories:** US-087, US-088, US-089
**Requirements:** REQ-IA-017, REQ-IA-018, REQ-IA-019

## Scenario AS-034.1 — Preview before settings change
**Given** a settings change affects navigation and outputs,
**When** the steward reviews the plan,
**Then** expected effects and the recovery approach are shown before execution.

## Scenario AS-034.2 — Bulk results remain itemized
**Given** a bulk operation has mixed results,
**When** it completes,
**Then** each item is recorded as successful, failed, skipped, retryable, or needing recovery.
