# User Stories

## Evidence and Decisions
- As a decision maker, I can inspect exact evidence, revisions, assumptions, competing interpretations, and confidence behind a Finding.
- As a reviewer, I can add a contradiction or alternative interpretation without overwriting another claim.

## Governed Reuse
- As a source owner, I can see inbound citations, inclusions, derivatives, and publications that depend on my Resource.
- As a destination owner, I can choose Live, Approval-Controlled, or Pinned adoption.
- As a reader, I can distinguish source content from adaptation, annotation, summary, and generated material.

## Trails
- As a Trail Author, I can create a versioned path through evidence, interpretations, decisions, work, and outcomes.
- As a reader, I can compare alternative Trails and views.

## Publication
- As a publisher, I can freeze dependencies to exact immutable Revisions before release.
- As an auditor, I can reconstruct sources, Relationships, transformations, approvals, tools, omissions, and configuration behind a Published Version.

## Governed History
- As a steward, I can withdraw, redact, restrict, or erase content under policy while preserving the minimum accountability record.
- As a reader, I am not misled into believing a filtered graph or answer is complete.

## Automation and Portability
- As a reviewer, I can identify automated contributions and inspect their authority, inputs, version, and limitations.
- As a system owner, I can export and independently reconstruct identities, Revisions, Relationships, Trails, provenance, and manifests.
