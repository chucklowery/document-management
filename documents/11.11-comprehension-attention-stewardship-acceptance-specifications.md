# Comprehension, Attention, and Stewardship Acceptance Specifications

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## Purpose

This document extends `11-acceptance-specifications.md` with observable behavior for human comprehension, attention governance, and stewardship cost.

# Feature AS-018 — Human Comprehension and Progressive Disclosure

**Stories:** US-049, US-050, US-051

**Requirements:** REQ-HT-024 through REQ-HT-028

## Scenario AS-018.1 — Explain a consequential conclusion

**Given** the system presents a consequential recommendation,

**When** an affected reader requests an explanation,

**Then** the system identifies material sources, applicable authority, assumptions, uncertainty, and generated time,

**And** distinguishes source facts, interpretations, Decisions, Projections, and generated material.

## Scenario AS-018.2 — Provide a simple initial view

**Given** a Resource has extensive provenance and Relationship detail,

**When** a reader opens the Resource,

**Then** the system presents a task-appropriate initial view,

**And** permits the reader to expand authorized provenance, Revision, Relationship, policy, and calculation details.

## Scenario AS-018.3 — Do not simplify away material risk

**Given** a conclusion depends on uncertain Evidence and scoped authority,

**When** the system presents a simplified explanation,

**Then** uncertainty and authority scope remain visible,

**And** the system does not imply greater certainty or broader authority than the source records support.

## Scenario AS-018.4 — Compare source and derivative

**Given** a Derivative Representation was created from exact source Revisions,

**When** an authorized reviewer compares it with the source,

**Then** the system identifies the exact source Revisions,

**And** describes material transformations, omissions, annotations, and automated contributions in understandable terms,

**And** keeps the derivative visibly distinguishable from the source.

# Feature AS-019 — Attention Governance

**Stories:** US-052, US-053, US-054

**Requirements:** REQ-HT-029 through REQ-HT-032

## Scenario AS-019.1 — Group related low-urgency notifications

**Given** several related low-urgency events occur within a policy-defined period,

**When** notification delivery is evaluated,

**Then** the system groups them according to the applicable attention policy,

**And** records the triggering events, grouping decision, recipients, and time.

## Scenario AS-019.2 — Suppress a superseded notification

**Given** a pending notification is made obsolete by a later event,

**When** the applicable policy permits suppression,

**Then** the system does not deliver the obsolete notification,

**And** records the suppression reason and superseding event.

## Scenario AS-019.3 — Escalate an unresolved urgent condition

**Given** an urgent condition remains unresolved beyond its policy threshold,

**When** escalation is evaluated,

**Then** the system notifies the next authorized audience according to policy,

**And** records urgency, threshold, escalation path, expected action, and time.

## Scenario AS-019.4 — Apply recipient preference prospectively

**Given** a recipient changes a permitted non-mandatory channel or digest preference,

**When** a later notification is evaluated,

**Then** the system applies the new preference,

**And** preserves mandatory policy-driven notices,

**And** does not treat the preference change as acknowledgement of an underlying obligation.

## Scenario AS-019.5 — Explain without leaking restricted trigger data

**Given** a notification was triggered by restricted information,

**When** a recipient requests an explanation,

**Then** the system explains the notification decision to the extent permitted by policy,

**And** does not disclose restricted trigger details, identifiers, or inference-enabling metadata.

# Feature AS-020 — Stewardship Cost and Staleness

**Stories:** US-055, US-056, US-057, US-058

**Requirements:** REQ-HT-033 through REQ-HT-039

## Scenario AS-020.1 — Assign stewardship to a consequential Resource

**Given** a Resource is designated authoritative or operationally consequential,

**When** stewardship is assigned,

**Then** the system records responsible Party, scope, effective period, and review policy,

**And** exposes when no active steward remains.

## Scenario AS-020.2 — Project staleness without rewriting source

**Given** a Resource satisfies an explicit staleness rule,

**When** current staleness is evaluated,

**Then** the system produces an explainable Staleness Projection,

**And** identifies the rule and source facts used,

**And** does not modify the Resource or assert that it is incorrect.

## Scenario AS-020.3 — Record stewardship review outcome

**Given** a steward reviews a potentially stale Resource,

**When** the review is completed,

**Then** the system records whether the Resource was reaffirmed, revised, superseded, withdrawn, archived, consolidated, or retained,

**And** records rationale, authority, and time.

## Scenario AS-020.4 — Assess downstream stewardship cost

**Given** a Source Owner is authorized to inspect inbound dependencies,

**When** a stewardship assessment is requested,

**Then** the system identifies permitted dependencies, review burden, sensitivity, and propagation impact,

**And** distinguishes measured values from estimates and policy-derived projections.

## Scenario AS-020.5 — Do not leak restricted dependencies through cost

**Given** some downstream dependencies are restricted,

**When** a stewardship assessment is presented to an unauthorized user,

**Then** the system does not reveal restricted dependencies through counts, identifiers, topology, or explanations.

## Scenario AS-020.6 — Retire information under policy

**Given** an authorized steward determines that information should be archived, withdrawn, consolidated, or retired,

**When** the governed action is recorded,

**Then** the system records rationale, authority, dependencies, retention policy, effective time, and resulting reference behavior,

**And** preserves or removes historical residue according to policy,

**And** does not fabricate replacement content.

## Scenario AS-020.7 — Cost does not establish truth

**Given** a Resource has high maintenance cost and low usage,

**When** stewardship policy evaluates it,

**Then** the system may recommend review or retirement,

**But** does not infer that the Resource is false, unauthorized, or disposable solely from cost or usage.
