# Attention Governance User Stories

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## Purpose

This document extends `08-user-stories.md` with dedicated stories for governed notification delivery and explainable attention policy.

## US-052 — Govern Notification Delivery

**As an Attention Policy Owner,** I want notification delivery governed by audience, urgency, frequency, grouping, suppression, escalation, and acknowledgement rules, so that consequential information reaches people without unbounded interruption.

**Owning context:** Identity, Access, and Governance

### Acceptance examples

- A notification identifies why the recipient is included and what action, if any, is expected.
- Related low-urgency events may be grouped into a digest.
- Duplicate or superseded notifications are suppressed according to policy.
- Urgent unresolved conditions may escalate according to explicit policy.

## US-053 — Control Personal Attention Preferences

**As a recipient,** I want to control permitted notification channels and non-mandatory frequency within policy, so that the system respects my attention while preserving required governance notices.

**Owning context:** Identity, Access, and Governance

### Acceptance examples

- The recipient can adjust non-mandatory channels and digest frequency.
- Mandatory legal, security, or approval notices remain governed by applicable policy.
- Preference changes are effective-dated and auditable where consequential.
- Disabling one channel does not acknowledge the underlying obligation.

## US-054 — Explain Notification Decisions

**As a recipient or auditor,** I want to understand why a notification was sent, grouped, suppressed, or escalated, so that attention policy is explainable.

**Owning context:** Identity, Access, and Governance

### Acceptance examples

- The system identifies the triggering event and applicable attention policy.
- The explanation identifies urgency, audience, grouping, suppression, and escalation decisions.
- Restricted triggering information is not disclosed through the explanation.
