# Continuity and Change Requirements

## REQ-IA-013 — Saved Workspace Revision
**EARS:** When a Saved Workspace is materially changed, the system shall preserve selected identities, Refinement State, view choices, owner, and review checkpoint in a new Revision.
**Stories:** US-082

## REQ-IA-014 — Cross-Channel Continuity
**EARS:** When a user follows a managed notification, export, or delivered output, the system shall restore or identify the related identity, filters, time boundary, and checkpoint where available.
**Stories:** US-084

## REQ-IA-015 — Categorized Change Review
**EARS:** When changes since a checkpoint are shown, the system shall distinguish additions, revisions, supersessions, withdrawals, relevance changes, stale items, failures, and unresolved items.
**Stories:** US-085
