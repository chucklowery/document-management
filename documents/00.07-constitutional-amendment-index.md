## Normative Rules

1. The Project Constitution and ratified amendments together form the constitutional authority for downstream artifacts.
2. A ratified amendment extends or constrains the Constitution without silently replacing unaffected principles.
3. Where two constitutional provisions conflict, the more specific ratified provision governs within its stated scope unless a later amendment states otherwise.
4. Supporting-needs documents, user stories, requirements, acceptance specifications, architecture decisions, implementations, and projections do not override the Constitution or a ratified amendment.
5. Every ratified amendment shall be reflected in the canonical glossary, domain model, context map, roles, stories, requirements, acceptance specifications, and architecture decisions where applicable.
6. Constitutional coverage shall be reported through the traceability ledger. Missing coverage is recorded as unimplemented rather than assumed satisfied.
