# Operational Narrative EARS Requirements

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## REQ-LO-015 — Operational Narrative

**EARS:** When a consequential operation is specified, the system shall maintain a versioned Operational Narrative explaining purpose, authority, preconditions, inputs, outputs, invariants, state transitions, failures, observability, governance, recovery, and examples in a human-comprehensible order.

**Stories:** US-098

## REQ-LO-016 — Narrative Links to Executable Definitions

**EARS:** When an Operational Narrative references a contract, schema, policy, state, check, example, dashboard, or recovery procedure, the system shall identify the exact Revision or durable selection rule and shall expose unresolved or stale references.

**Stories:** US-098, US-099

## REQ-LO-017 — Narrative Conformance Result

**EARS:** When documentation validation runs, the system shall produce a Narrative Conformance Result identifying the Narrative Revision, evaluated boundary, checks, mismatches, time, environment, and evidence.

**Stories:** US-099
