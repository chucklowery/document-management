# Feature AS-036 — Safe Contract Evolution

**Stories:** US-091, US-092, US-101  
**Requirements:** REQ-LO-001, REQ-LO-002, REQ-LO-003, REQ-LO-004, REQ-LO-019

## Scenario AS-036.1 — Unsupported Revision is visible

**Given** a channel accepts identified Contract Revisions,  
**When** information arrives under an unsupported Revision,  
**Then** the system rejects, quarantines, or negotiates it according to policy,  
**And** records the received Revision and outcome.

## Scenario AS-036.2 — Breaking change requires migration evidence

**Given** a proposed Contract Revision changes consumer-visible meaning,  
**When** compatibility and conformance are evaluated,  
**Then** adoption is blocked until affected consumers have an approved migration or time-bounded exception,  
**And** received time semantics remain reconstructable.
