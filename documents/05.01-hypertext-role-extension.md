# Hypertext Role Extension

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## Purpose

This document extends `05-user-roles.md` with contextual responsibilities introduced by the Hypertext Knowledge Integrity Amendment. These roles supplement rather than replace the existing role catalog.

## Source Author

Creates and revises authoritative source content and identifies intended meaning, assumptions, evidence, and applicable context.

## Source Owner

Governs an authoritative source Resource within a defined scope, including Revision policy, reuse policy, downstream impact, correction, withdrawal, restriction, stewardship, and retention expectations.

## Destination Author

Uses citations, references, inclusions, transclusions, adaptations, summaries, or annotations in a destination Resource while preserving source identity and distinguishing source content from adaptation.

## Destination Owner

Controls adoption of source changes into a destination Resource and accepts responsibility for destination context. Selects Pinned, Live, Approval-Controlled, or Historical-As-Of adoption according to policy.

## Synthesizer

Creates Findings, summaries, comparisons, interpretations, or generated knowledge products from multiple sources while preserving sources, transformations, material omissions, disagreement, and uncertainty.

## Trail Author

Creates and maintains a versioned Trail for a stated audience and purpose, including ordered steps, selection modes, rationale, alternatives, and limitations.

## Trail Reviewer

Reviews an exact Trail Revision for accuracy, completeness claims, source selection, omissions, authorization, and fitness for purpose.

## Trail Adoption Authority

Adopts an exact Trail Revision for a defined organizational, educational, decision, audit, or publication scope. Adoption does not establish universal truth.

## Auditor

Reconstructs historical states, Decisions, publications, derivations, Trails, access-sensitive actions, and verification evidence.

## Consent Grantor

Controls applicable consent for collection, retention, reuse, disclosure, portrayal, or downstream processing.

## Portability Steward

Maintains documented export and reconstruction capability while preserving semantic meaning and enforcing applicable governance restrictions.

## Attention Policy Owner

Governs notifications, dependency alerts, adoption requests, approval requests, automated recommendations, and escalation policy.

## Relationship to Existing Roles

- Source Author may also hold Author or Evidence Contributor.
- Source Owner may also hold Resource Steward.
- Destination Author may also hold Editor or Analyst.
- Destination Owner may also hold Project Owner, Editor, or Publication Authority.
- Synthesizer may also hold Analyst or Learning Author.
- Trail Reviewer may also hold Knowledge Reviewer.
- Trail Adoption Authority may also hold Decision Maker or Publication Authority.
- Auditor should remain distinguishable from System Operator and Access Administrator.
- Consent authority is not implied by Source Ownership.

## Separation-of-Duties Candidates

Policy may require separation between Trail Author and Trail Adoption Authority, Synthesizer and final Decision Maker, System Operator and Auditor, or Automated Agent and final approval authority.
