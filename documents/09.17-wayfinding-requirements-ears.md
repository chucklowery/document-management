# Wayfinding EARS Requirements

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## REQ-IA-001 — Wayfinding Context
**EARS:** When a view is presented, the system shall identify active scope, current identity and Revision or selection rule, temporal boundary, applied constraints, and available next paths.
**Stories:** US-076

## REQ-IA-002 — Complementary Discovery Paths
**EARS:** Where applicable paths exist, the system shall permit movement among identity lookup, search, browse, facets, Relationships, backlinks, Trails, chronology, and Saved Workspaces.
**Stories:** US-077

## REQ-IA-003 — Contextual Navigation Labels
**EARS:** When a navigation label is shown, the system shall apply the label and definition for the applicable audience, language, and bounded context.
**Stories:** US-076, US-078

## REQ-IA-004 — Stable Navigable Address
**EARS:** When a user resumes a managed navigation address, the system shall resolve the intended identity, Revision or selection rule, and permitted view rather than display text alone.
**Stories:** US-076, US-084
