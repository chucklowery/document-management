# Literate Operations Role Extension

**Project:** Organizational Knowledge and Work System

## Roles

- **Channel Steward:** maintains channel ownership, lifecycle state, active contracts, dependencies, and service objectives.
- **Contract Steward:** maintains contract Revisions, compatibility assessments, migration policy, and conformance evidence.
- **Consumer Steward:** states a consumer boundary's required meaning and records adoption decisions.
- **Batch Operator:** runs bounded batch, replay, backfill, resume, and synchronization work and reviews item outcomes.
- **Capacity Operator:** observes backlog and applies the declared admission policy.
- **Consistency Auditor:** records Drift Findings and verifies Repair Runs.
- **Operational Narrator:** maintains the human-readable operation description and links to contracts, checks, dashboards, and recovery evidence.
- **Migration Steward:** governs breaking-change migration and completion.
- **Sunset Steward:** governs deprecation after affected consumers and pending work are addressed.

Each consequential action records the role and scope used. Role assignment does not transfer domain authority.
